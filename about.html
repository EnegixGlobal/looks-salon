30 Years of Excellence

What began as a single salon in the heart of Delhi in 1993 has grown into India's most trusted premium grooming brand. Founded on the belief that every individual deserves a world-class salon experience, Looks Salon has redefined beauty standards across the nation.

Today, with over 200 outlets spanning 55+ cities, we continue our mission — delivering an unmatched blend of artistry, luxury, and care to every single client who walks through our doors.

Expansion to 10+ cities. Looks becomes the first professional salon chain in India with a scalable franchise model.

Crossed 100 premium outlets across India. Launched the Looks Loyalty Programme to reward our most valued clients.
